fxCheckout is a helper that allows you to checkout a hudson.plugins.git.GitSCM object and fetch some information about it.

Usage:
  scmInfo = fxCheckout(
    scm: hudson.plugins.git.GitSCM <optionnal>
  )
  println scmInfo.commitId
  println scmInfo.branch
  println scmInfo.tag
  println scmInfo.isPullRequest
